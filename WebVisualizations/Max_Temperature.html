Max Temperature Overview

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the souther hemishpere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemishpere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the fime of the year this data was gathered.
